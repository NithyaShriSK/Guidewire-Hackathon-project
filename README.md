📌 Executive Summary

GigShield is an AI-powered parametric insurance platform designed to protect gig workers—specifically Amazon Flex delivery partners—from income loss caused by real-world disruptions like weather, pollution, and traffic.

Unlike traditional insurance:

❌ No claim filing

❌ No manual verification

❌ No delays

👉 GigShield uses a Zero-Touch Model:
When predefined environmental conditions are met, payouts are triggered automatically and instantly.
